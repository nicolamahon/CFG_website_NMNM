Welcome to FindMyRoomie!

Our Mission

Aiding the search for the perfect roommate
to enable the perfect college experience

At FindMyRoomie, we aim to find you the perfect roommate and accommodation to make your college experience as enjoyable as possible. We aim to match you with prospective roommates who have similar personality traits to you, and then find you housing to suit your needs and funds. College is a time to enjoy your newfound freedom and make lifelong friends, so we would like to take the stress out of finding a place to live and finding awesome roomies! If you would like to sign up to FMR, please don’t hesitate to become a Roomie!

About Us
FindMyRoomie was developed to make the transition from family life to student life as easy as possible. When we (Nicola and Nollaig) were starting college, we found it difficult to find accommodation, and the prospect of living with complete strangers was absolutely daunting! Thankfully we both struggled through (after many bad decisions), and settled into the busy life of college students. From personal experiences we have learned that no-one has the ability to like and ‘get on’ with everyone, and landlords, they can take liberties with the truth. We aim to act as a filter, from landlord to tenant, from roommate to roommate, and extract the best options tailored to your needs and personality. Hopefully you will enjoy your FMR experience and make lifelong friends along the way.

We look forward to hearing from you all very soon!
